Privacy Policy — Signal Scoop

Last updated: May 20, 2026
Publisher: Manticore Technologies, LLC
App: Signal Scoop (com.signalsoop.app)

Summary

Signal Scoop scans local wireless signals on your phone. Scan results and saved history stay on your device. We do not receive your scans on our servers. The app uses HTTPS only when you optionally download an on-device AI model, and when the Knowledge map loads background map tiles (no scan data is sent in tile requests).

Information the app accesses

When you tap Scan nearby signals:

DataPurposeSent to publisher?
BLE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth survey dataShow and save resultsNo
NFC, sensorsShow capability / hardwareNo
Location / GPSAndroid scan rules; optional coordinates saved with scansNo (local only)

Stored on your device

  • Saved scans and findings
  • Knowledge graph (map of scans, signals, places)
  • Optional aliases, photos, videos, notes, mesh keys

Delete scans in the app or uninstall to remove all data.

What we do not do

  • No accounts
  • No sale of personal data
  • No cloud backup of scan history in the stock app
  • No upload of scan or graph data to Manticore servers

Network

Optional model download (HTTPS, user-initiated) and map tile loading (HTTPS to map provider) only. Scan payloads are not transmitted to us.
