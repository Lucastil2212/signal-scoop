Privacy Policy — Signal Scoop

Last updated: May 19, 2026
App: Signal Scoop (com.signalsoop.app)

Summary

Signal Scoop scans local wireless signals on your phone. All processing happens on your device. We do not operate servers that receive your scan results, and the app does not use internet access.

What we do not collect

  • No analytics or ads in the stock app
  • No sale of personal data
  • No cloud backup of scan results

Retention

Results are kept in memory during your session and cleared when you leave the app.
